1. Introduction

Me and my group are going to make a website about the topic cars.

We will make a comparison between electrical and compustion engines cars. Our web application is a survey where you have to answer a few questions and then some cars will be suggested that best suit your needs.

We will show you some electrical and combustion engine cars.

2. Comparison

The comparison will be just a normal table.

We will have to columns. On the left side we will talk about electrical cars, the good and the bad things about it.

On the other side we will talk about the same things just about combustion engine cars.

At last we will give our opinion about it and what we think about both subthemes.

3. The survey

The survey will be our web application.

In this survey you will be asked some questions that you have to answer.

These questions will be processed and at the end 2-3 cars will be suggested to you that best fit your needs.

4. Cars

We will show you some electrical and combustion engine cars from every different price class.
